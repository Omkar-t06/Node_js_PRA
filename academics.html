Academics

B tech

Our engineering programs offer a comprehensive curriculum that blends theoretical knowledge with practical skills. Students have the opportunity to specialize in various branches such as Computer Science, Information Technology, Electronics & Communication, and Mechanical Engineering.

Computer Science

Our Computer Science program offers in-depth knowledge of programming, algorithms, databases, and software engineering. Students learn to develop cutting-edge applications and solutions for various industries.

Information Technology

The Information Technology program focuses on the practical application of technology to solve business problems. Students learn about network administration, web development, database management, and more.

Artificial Intelligence / Machine Learning

Our AI/ML program equips students with the skills to design intelligent systems that can learn from data. Topics covered include machine learning algorithms, deep learning, natural language processing, and computer vision.

Data Science

The Data Science program teaches students how to analyze large datasets to extract valuable insights. Students learn statistical methods, data visualization techniques, and machine learning algorithms.

Cyber Security

Our Cyber Security program focuses on protecting information systems from cyber threats. Students learn about network security, cryptography, ethical hacking, and digital forensics.

Electronics & Communication Engineering

The ECE program covers a wide range of topics including electronics, digital signal processing, communication systems, and embedded systems. Students learn to design and implement electronic devices and communication networks.
